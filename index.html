So basically this game is an exact clone of the popular game Wordle.

If you're not familiar here is a run down of the game:

Your goal is to guess the secret word, to find the word you have to guess letters or words in the boxes going across to the right.

Game mechanics:
If the letter shows as yellow, that means the letter is in the secret word but not in that spot, if the letter shows green that means the letter is in the secret word and in the correct spot. If the colour doesn't change at all, that means the letter is not at all in the word!
